FAQ

Answers to common questions.

What Shopify themes do you work with?

We work with modern, well-supported Shopify themes and can advise on the right starting point. We also support custom and heavily modified themes.

Do you work with existing stores?

Yes. Many projects start with an existing store that needs structure, speed, or UX improvements rather than a full rebuild.

Can you improve speed and Core Web Vitals?

Yes. We run performance audits, clean up apps and scripts, optimize assets, and adjust templates to improve real-world loading and interaction.

Do you offer accessibility support?

Yes. We offer accessibility audits and remediation to bring templates closer to WCAG guidelines and improve keyboard and screen reader support.

What do you need from me to start?

A quick overview of your brand, goals, current theme, key URLs, and timing. Access to your Shopify store comes after we agree on scope.

What is the typical timeline?

Smaller landing page projects often run 2–4 weeks. Store refreshes and new builds usually range from 4–8 weeks depending on scope.
